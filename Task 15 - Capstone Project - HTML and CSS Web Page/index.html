"Where Big Hearts and Big Paws Play Freely!"

Bolos Doggy Daycare is a haven tailored for our furry companions, specifically designed with large breed dogs in mind. Nestled in the heart of a bustling community, it offers a nurturing environment where these majestic canines can thrive.

What sets Bolos apart is its inclusive approach, welcoming dogs without the requirement of being neutered. This ethos stems from a deep understanding of canine behavior and the belief that each dog deserves to socialize and play freely.

Here, every wagging tail and playful bark is celebrated, creating a vibrant atmosphere filled with joy and camaraderie. At Bolos, it's more than just a daycare—it's a home away from home where big dogs can roam, explore, and forge lasting friendships under the watchful eye of dedicated caretakers.

Our Services

Grooming

Our grooming services offer tailored care to keep your furry friend looking and feeling their best. From luxurious baths to breed-specific trims, our expert groomers provide gentle, stress-free treatments. It's not just grooming—it's our commitment to your dog's well-being and happiness.

Walking

Bolos Daycare provides top-notch walking services for your beloved canine companions. Our experienced walkers ensure that every outing is not just a stroll, but an adventure tailored to your dog's energy level and preferences. Whether it's a leisurely neighborhood jaunt or an exhilarating romp through the park, our walkers prioritize safety, exercise, and fun. With Bolos, your dog will enjoy stimulating walks that keep them happy, healthy, and wagging their tails with joy.

Training

Bolos Daycare offers comprehensive training services to help your dog reach their full potential. Our expert trainers employ positive reinforcement techniques to teach essential obedience commands, address behavioral issues, and foster a strong bond between you and your furry friend. From puppy basics to advanced skills, our personalized approach ensures that each training session is tailored to your dog's unique needs and learning style. With Bolos, you'll see remarkable progress and enjoy a harmonious relationship with your well-trained companion.

Socializing

Socialization is a key aspect of the experience offered to furry friends. Through supervised play sessions, dogs have the opportunity to interact with other dogs in a safe and controlled environment. This socialization helps dogs build confidence, learn proper behavior, and develop important social skills. Additionally, trained staff members facilitate positive interactions and ensure that all dogs are comfortable and having fun. Overall, Bolos doggy daycare prioritizes socialization as a vital component of promoting a happy and well-adjusted pet.
