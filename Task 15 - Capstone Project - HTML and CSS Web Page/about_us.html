Our Story

We're more than just a daycare; we're a community dedicated to providing top-notch care and fun-filled days for your furry family members. Founded by Brett, a passionate dog lover and proud owner of Bolo, our daycare was born out of a genuine need for quality care for large breed dogs like Bolo. As a Rottweiler hailing from rich German bloodlines, Bolo faced challenges in finding daycare options that catered to his size and breed. Determined to provide a solution, Brett embarked on the journey of creating Bolos Doggy Daycare.

Our mission is simple: to offer a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where dogs of all shapes and sizes can thrive. Whether your pup is here for a day of play, socialization, or overnight stays, rest assured they'll receive the same love and attention as Bolo himself.

What sets us apart:

  • Tailored Care: We understand that every dog is unique, which is why we take the time to get to know each furry friend individually. From dietary needs to play preferences, we tailor our care to suit your dog's specific requirements.
  • Expert Staff: Our team consists of experienced and passionate dog lovers who undergo extensive training in canine behavior, first aid, and safety protocols. Your dog is in good hands with us!
  • Spacious Facilities: Our state-of-the-art facility boasts ample indoor and outdoor space for dogs to roam, play, and socialize. Whether it's a game of fetch in the yard or a cozy nap indoors, there's plenty of room for every pup to enjoy.
  • Enriching Activities: From interactive toys to group play sessions, we offer a variety of enriching activities designed to keep your dog mentally and physically stimulated throughout their stay.
  • Community Atmosphere: At Bolos, we believe in fostering a sense of community among our clients and their canine companions. Whether it's our regular Yappy Hours or special events, we love bringing dogs and their humans together for fun and camaraderie.

Nutritious Meals:

At Bolos Doggy Daycare, we understand the importance of a balanced diet in keeping your canine companion healthy and happy. That's why we provide high-quality, nutritious meals for our furry guests. We carefully select premium dog foods that meet the dietary needs of dogs of all ages, sizes, and breeds. From puppyhood to senior years, we ensure that your dog receives the nourishment they need to thrive. We proudly partner with Royal Canin, a trusted leader in pet nutrition, to offer a range of specially formulated diets tailored to support your dog's unique needs.
